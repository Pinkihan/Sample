Who are we?

We believe that everything starts with an idea and for Out Of Line; the idea was inspired by our founders’ love for Coffee. Out Of Line is the ultimate haven for Coffee lovers in Riyadh. Now one of the country’s top destination where many delight in signature coffee and desserts made with high quality ingredients as well as other savory treats all of which enhance customers’ superior experience.

Out Of Line complements their array of dessert with a selection of premium offerings such as an exquisite breakfast menu, savory selection, freshly baked pastries and an assortment of beverages.
